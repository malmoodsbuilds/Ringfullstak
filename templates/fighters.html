Terence 'Bud' Crawford

42-0

The current Pound for Pound king. The only 3 division Undisputed Champion in the 4 belt era, Known for his unrivaled boxing IQ and killer instinct, Bud is widely considered as one of the greatest fighters ever. Becoming the first ever undisputed Super lightweight champion in boxing history, Still Bud had many detractors when moving up to the welterweight division, With all speculation being put to rest as Bud cleaned out the division and left it with an emphatic 8 round destruction against longtime rival Errol Spence Jr which made Bud a 2 division undisputed champion. This clearly was not enough for the Omaha native as he further stamped his greatness with his emphatic win over fellow HOF Saul Alvarez moving up 2 divisions to become undisputed super middleweight king, the only man in boxing history to accomplish this feat and the second man to become 3 weight undisputed alongside legend Henry Armstrong




Saul 'Canelo' Alvarez

63-3(39KOs)

Canelo Alvarez, Known for many years as one of the biggest fighters in boxing and for good reason. With a jam packed resume and right hand, Alvarez has won titles in 4 divisions becoming the first undisputed super middleweight in boxing history. This along with his victories in his legendary trilogy with Gennady Golovkin as well as the stunning KO of Sergey Kovalev in his move up to light heavyweight is a primary reason Alvarez has remained one of the most popular fighters today.
